About

Teacher

Inderjit Singh Kang owner and coordinator for punjabi bhangra and cultural academy. He is teaching bhangra from 2006 in Canada.

Bhangra is his passion and is attached to bhangra since his school time.

Who we are ?

We started Punjabi Bhangra and Cultural academy in 2015 at Brantford. then in 2018 we started our second location in Brampton.

We teach thousands of students through Punjabi bhangra and cultural academy in Brantford and Brampton as well.

Competitions

We organise iinter-academy competition (Nachde Punjabi Bhangra Festivals) twice a year. Also we give opportunity to our students to compete with other teams in North america and world wide.
